# Citation

If you use this pipeline or its analysis code in your research, please cite:

## Primary paper

> Shah S, et al. (2023). Weighted gene co-expression network analysis of
> developmental brain transcriptomics reveals connectivity patterns of 22q13
> deletion syndrome genes associated with autism spectrum disorder,
> intellectual disability, seizures, hypotonia, and language impairment.

> https://doi.org/10.3390/genes14111998

> Langfelder P, Horvath S (2008). WGCNA: an R package for weighted
> correlation network analysis. *BMC Bioinformatics* **9**: 559.
> https://doi.org/10.1186/1471-2105-9-559

> Miller JA, et al. (2014). Transcriptional landscape of the prenatal
